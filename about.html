Empowering Students with Knowledge and Skills

Capital Highway Training College (CHTC) is committed to providing industry-relevant education and practical skills to empower students for career success.

  • Established in 2022, CHTC offers diploma, certificate, and artisan courses in various disciplines.
  • We emphasize hands-on training and competency-based learning to meet industry standards.
  • Our multidisciplinary team of professionals ensures a well-rounded educational experience for all students.
