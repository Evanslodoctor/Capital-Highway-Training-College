Entry Requirements

General Admission Criteria

Diploma Courses

Minimum KCSE grade of C- (minus) or equivalent qualification depending on course and exam body.

Certificate Courses

Minimum KCSE grade of D (plain). Some courses accept D- (minus) depending on level and examining body.

Artisan & Short Courses

Open to all applicants. No formal academic qualifications required.

TVETA-CDACC Levels

Level 4: KCSE D- and above
Level 5: KCSE D and above
Level 6: KCSE C- and above

Specialized Courses

Courses like programming, web design, and computer packages are open to all – no entry requirements.

Mature Entry

Consideration is given to applicants with relevant work experience or informal training.

Fee Information

Tuition Breakdown & Payment Structure

Annual Fee Structure

Most students pay fees for Term One and Term Two only (Term Three is free). Diploma and Level 6 students pay tuition fees in the third term (amount varies by course).

Per Term Fees

Fees range between Ksh 16,000 and Ksh 22,500 per term depending on the course and level. Diploma/Level 6 third term fees vary by specific course.

Payment Options

Flexible payment plans are available. Students can pay in installments before the end of each term (including Diploma/Level 6 third term fees).

What Fees Cover

Fees cover tuition, learning materials, access to facilities, and practical sessions depending on the course.

Additional Charges

Some courses may have extra costs for exams (e.g., KNEC, NITA, CDACC) or Class and project materials/products.

Fee Payment Schedule

Fees for Term One and Two must be cleared before the start of each term. Diploma/Level 6 students pay third term tuition fees only.
