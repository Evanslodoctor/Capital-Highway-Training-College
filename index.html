About Capital Highway Training College

Established in 2022, Capital Highway Training College is dedicated to equipping students with hands-on, industry-relevant skills for a competitive job market.

  • Offering diploma, certificate, and artisan courses across various disciplines.
  • Modern facilities and practical learning to meet industry standards.
  • A multidisciplinary team of professionals committed to academic excellence and skill development.
